Q: Do hamsters provide food for any animals?
A: Let's think step by step.
Step1: Hamsters are prey animals.
Step2: Prey are food for predators.
Step3: Thus, hamsters provide food for some animals.
Step4: So the answer is \boxed{(yes)}.
